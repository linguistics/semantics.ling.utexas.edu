Links

  • The www.liwc.net website is a public purchasing and information site for one of the primary software used in this grant project. The www.utpsyc.org website includes demonstrations of study protocols used in collecting language data.

    Also: www.wordwatchers.wordpress.org

    The Linguistic Inquiry and Word Count (LIWC) product is a word counting software that is made up of a processor and a dictionary of grammatical, psychological, and content categories in English. Dictionaries with grammatical, psychological, and content categories have been developed in other languages for use with LIWC. Modern Standard Arabic LIWC dictionaries were completed. Chinese, French, Persian, Russian, and Turkish LIWC dictionaries are in various stages of development.

    LIWC is available for purchase on www.liwc.net. The software is freely shared between collaborators on this grant. The LIWC dictionaries are available to academic researchers by request for non-commercial purposes.
